Travel Expenses Tracker is a simple and convenient app for tracking expenses during travels.

🎯 KEY FEATURES:
• Create trips with names and dates
• Add expenses with category selection
• Automatic total cost calculation
• Detailed statistics by category
• Beautiful and intuitive interface

📊 EXPENSE CATEGORIES:
• 🍽️ Food - restaurants, cafes, groceries
• 🚗 Transport - taxis, buses, trains
• 🏠 Accommodation - hotels, hostels, rentals
• 🎮 Entertainment - museums, tours, activities

✨ FEATURES:
• Automatic data saving
• Color-coded categories
• Modern SwiftUI design
• Works on iPhone and iPad
• No internet required

Perfect for travelers who want to control their budget and track expenses during trips.

Download Travel Expenses Tracker and start planning your travels wisely! 🌍✈️
